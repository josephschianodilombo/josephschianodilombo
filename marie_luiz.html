Marie Luiz, Autorretrato

2017
Marie-Louise en carton blanc
70 x 100 cm

Autoportrait d’une artiste de la péninsule onirique : Marie Luiz. Il s’agit également d’un écho dans la forme à l’œuvre Erased De Kooning Drawing" de Rauschenberg.

in French, "Marie-Louise" is not only a name, the word also refers to the mount used in framing. By giving the mount the entire space allowed by the frame, "Marie-Louise" becomes the central subject. It is also an echo of Rauschenberg's Erased De Kooning Drawing.
